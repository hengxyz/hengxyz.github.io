  • MIDV-2020:

    • A Comprehensive Benchmark Dataset for Identity Document Analysis. You can download the dataset here.

    MIDV-2020 comprises 10 document types, which consists of 1000 annotated video clips, 1000 scanned images, and 1000 photos of 1000 unique mock identity documents, each with unique text field values and unique artificially generated faces.
